About me

I received my B.S. degree from Southwest Petroleum University in 2019.
I received my Ph.D. degree from Beijing Normal University in 2025.
I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interests focus on computer vision, especially low-level vision tasks.
